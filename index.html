New skill

What is a lower third?

A lower third is the animated graphic that appears at the bottom of the screen during an interview, showing the person's name and title. You see these on news channels, documentaries, and YouTube videos all the time. You'll build yours from scratch inside DaVinci Resolve's Fusion page.

Steps to create it

  1. 1Select your interview clip on the timeline and open the Fusion page
  2. 2Add a Text+ node — type the interviewee's name and role
  3. 3Add a Background node with a colored rectangle behind the text
  4. 4Use a Merge node to combine text, background, and video
  5. 5Add keyframes to animate the position or opacity so it slides/fades in and out
